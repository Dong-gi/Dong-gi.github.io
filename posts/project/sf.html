Terms

  1. SFNode
    • Proxy object of other object(Usually HTMLElement instance)
    • Returned by SF.build, SF.asSF, SF.asSFarr
    • Original object can be accessed by SFNode.$
  2. App instance

    3. SFNode returned by SF.build
